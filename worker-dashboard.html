📌 Always Remember
🌵 DRY SEASON WATER ALERT
November to March — fresh water must be checked and refilled every single day. This is a mandatory logged task. A nursing or pregnant female without water is an emergency.
🤰Watch for pregnancy signs — visible belly from Month 3, weight gain, nesting behaviour. Log the expected birth date immediately.
🍼Birth occurred? 72-hour no-disturb protocol. Do not touch pups. Double feed and water for the nursing female — she is feeding herself and 5–8 hybrid pups. Log pup count at birth and at 24 hours.
♂️Hybrid growing males must be separated by 12 weeks — move them at 10 weeks before fighting starts. Hybrid males are larger and stronger — their fights kill. One male per grow-out slot, no exceptions.
🐕No dogs or cats within 5 metres of any cage. Their scent alone causes panic and stress in the whole colony — even if the animals never see them.
🔇Move slowly, speak quietly near maternity cages always. Loud noise or sudden movement near a new mother can cause her to harm her pups.
🔴Any RED health status — notify the owner the same day. Do not wait for the weekly report on Sunday.

📋 Weekly Report
🐾 Log a Colony Event
Only log when something actually happens — a birth, death, isolation, male movement, maternity entry, weaning, or backdated entry request.
All animals on this farm are hybrid. No breed field needed.
Take a photo of the animal immediately. If disease is suspected, notify the owner TODAY — do not wait for the weekly report. Submit one entry per animal.
Use this after a death when a new animal is placed into the vacant slot. The old ID is retired; the new animal gets the next available ID.
Mark an animal as isolated (sick, aggressive, post-birth recovery). Owner sees this flagged immediately.
Use this every time the breeding male moves from one batch to another. The system will calculate his expected removal date and the expected birth date for the batch he just entered.
Log when a pregnant female moves into a maternity cage. She stays until pups are 6 weeks old. Grease the cage hinges before she enters — silent door operation is required.
Log when pups are weaned at 6 weeks and moved from the maternity cage to the weaning cage. Male and female pups must be separated within the weaning cage before 10 weeks.
Log when a growing male is moved into an individual grow-out slot. Must happen before 12 weeks — do not wait until fighting starts. One male per slot, no exceptions.
Only use this if your phone was unavailable and you kept a physical logbook. The owner must approve this request before you can log missed days. The approval window expires 24 hours after it is granted.
